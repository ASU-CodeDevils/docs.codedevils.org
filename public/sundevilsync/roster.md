Roster
======
The roster is used to manage CodeDevils membership. You can add/remove/modify memberships, manage officer positions,
and even send messages via email or text.

Disclaimer: members names will not appear in any example images to protect their anonymity.

## Navigate to the Action Center
Navigate to the action center by following [this link](https://asu.campuslabs.com/engage/actioncenter/organization/codedevils)
or by navigating to the [organization home page](https://asu.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/codedevils) and selecting the
**Manage Organization** button in the top right. You must be a registered officer to access the action center.

##### Position Fields
Fill out the fields as appropriate:
* **Position Name**: The name of the position as it will appear in SunDevilSync.
* **Position Type**: Can be either _Member_ or _Officer_.
* **Show holders of this position on the organization's public roster**: The position and the associated member will
appear on the [home page](https://asu.campuslabs.com/engage/organization/codedevils).
* **Active**: If left unchecked, the position will only be available as a Past Position, meaning the position is cannot
be assigned to the active holder of the position.
* **Set Management Access**: Access to the [organization action center dashboard](https://asu.campuslabs.com/engage/actioncenter/organization/codedevils)
can be granted through one of three tiers:
    * **_No Access_** - The member cannot access organization action center.
    * **_All Access_** - The member has view and edit access to organization action center.
    * **_Limited Access_** - You can assigned individual view/edit access to the different parts of organization action center.
    
Select **CREATE** to finish creating the position.

### Messaging

Although Slack is the preferred method for communicating with members, SunDevilSync offers messaging for sending out
mass messages by either [email](#email) or [SMS](#sms).

#### Email

If the relay you are needing already exists, skip to [Send a Relay Email Message](#send-a-relay-email-message).
