What about gifts?

Giving recognition items

- ASU doesn't give "gifts". We give recognition items.
- Recognition items are given to recognize someone for service or leadership.
- When giving recognition items, your organization must record the following items. You are welcome to use this [Recognition Item Form](https://fso.engineering.asu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2013/03/Recognition-Item-Form.docx) t ensure you get everything necessary.
  - Date the item was received
  - Specific item(s) received
  - Recipient&#39;s printed name
  - Recipient&#39;s signature
- You must submit this information with your paperwork.
- The p-card CANNOT be used to purchase recognition items.
