# Event-planning policies

## Planning an event

Planning an event? Does the event involve:

- Controversial topics, elected officials
- VIP attendance
- International guests
- High attendance
- Safety risks, such as tents, canopies, lots of tables and chairs, large rental equipment, explosives
- K12 events with kids on campus
- Organization career fairs
- Fulton Student council org fair
- Hosting an org conference
- Inflatable party
- VIP speaker for large event
- Hosting an org competition

## Submit the online registration form

- If you answered yes to any of the previous options, you&#39;ll submit the online event form
  - Go Here: [http://outreach.asu.edu/events/registration](http://outreach.asu.edu/events/registration)
  - Sign in using your ASURite ID in the upper right hand corner
  - Provide all required information
  - Verify you have received a confirmation email with correct information
- If it is just a meeting with food, you do not need to submit the form

## Online event form notes

- Submitting the form does not reserve your meeting space for you. You must submit reservations in addition to this form
- The collected data will be distributed to the proper individuals or departments to assist you in any safety or protocol measures. If anything needs to be addressed or additional permits are needed, they will be in c
- Failure to fill this form out could result in cancellation of the event due to lack of required permitting or security procedures in place
