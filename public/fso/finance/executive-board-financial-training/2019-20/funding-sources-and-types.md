# Funding sources and types

## FSO funding options

Fulton Student Organizations may seek/manage funding from a variety of sources. A few resources to explore:

- Need funding for a big community event? Look into [ASU Pitchfunder](http://pitchfunder.asufoundation.org/). Use [this worksheet](https://fso.engineering.asu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2016/09/PitchFunder-Checklist.xlsx)before diving in.
- [Fundraising tips](https://fso.engineering.asu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2016/07/FundsFundraising_Handout.pdf) from ASU Student Organization Support
- Organization off-campus bank account
- [Undergraduate Student Government Polytechnic (USGP)](http://www.usgpoly.com/about.html)
- [Undergraduate Student Government Tempe (USG)](https://www.asuusg.com/)
- [Graduate Professional Student Association (GPSA)](https://gpsa.asu.edu/funding/)
- [FSE: Dean&#39;s Funding](https://studentorgs.engineering.asu.edu/deans-funding/)
- Soliciting industry or individual donations (more information provided in the Donation section of the training)
- Org initiatives: dues, national organization support, book sales, FSO eat nights, garage sales, car washes, etc.

## Organization off-campus bank accounts

- FSOs may open an off-campus bank account (i.e. not an ASU-managed account).
- Off-campus accounts can be difficult to transfer to new owners/leaders, depending on initial bank account terms.
- Make sure your president, treasurer and advisor has access to the bank account.
- Fulton Student Engagement **DOES NOT** process FSO&#39;s funds requests or reimbursements for off-campus organizations bank accounts.

## FSO Fulton accounts

- FSO&#39;s may have multiple on-campus Fulton accounts for different types of funding. If an FSO does not currently have an account, it can be created if your organization is awarded funding that needs an account.
- Funds requests and reimbursements are approved by the Student Engagement office and processed by the Business Office
- When requesting to spend funding from any of your accounts, carefully provide the required account numbers.

## Donation policies

- FSOs are encouraged to seek corporate donations to help support FSO activities. FSOs can review the Phoenix Business Journal [Book of Lists.](http://studentorgs.engineering.asu.edu/toolbox)
-  to see the various companies per industry in the Valley and reach out to relevant companies for support.
- Individuals or corporations can give donations
- All donations must include a donation letter or email stating name, company, amount, who the funds are for, and what the funds can be used for.
- FSOs are encouraged to ask donors to submit their donations through the ASU Foundation. By doing so, the ASU Foundation can follow up with the donor for tax-deductible information, track who&#39;s supporting ASU and recognize donors.
- Upon receiving the donation, the funds will be transferred to the FSO&#39;s donation account (managed within Student Engagement). The process may take 30-45 days from the date the donation was deposited.
