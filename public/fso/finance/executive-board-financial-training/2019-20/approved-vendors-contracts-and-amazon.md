# Approved vendors, contracts and Amazon

## Use ASU-approved vendors

- Certain vendors have ASU-friendly insurance to serve our community. Always use them.
- For all food/beverage-related purchases: use the [ASU-Approved Food Providers](https://eoss.asu.edu/mu/plan_event/food).
- Always check the [ASU SunMart list](https://cfo.asu.edu/sunmart) for contracted and preferred vendors for big purchases (such as car rentals, buses, etc.).

## Buying clothing

- Always check the [ASU-approved promotional items list](https://cfo.asu.edu/purchasing-sunmart-promo).
- Use them for all purchases (p-card, purchase order and reimbursements!)
- Ask the Vendor to refer to their ASU RFP contract 1501501 with quotes and invoicing

## Signing contracts

If your FSO has a contract to review, we strongly encourage you to review it with your FSO advisor prior to signing it. Be aware of these policies:

- If you sign any contracts either with ASU or an outside vendor, **you MUST UPHOLD the contract terms.** Before signing, be sure you can fulfill the terms.
- You may NOT sign contracts on ASU&#39;s behalf.**
- DO NOT sign contracts over $5,000.**

## Amazon purchases

- ASU does not allow personal credit cards as a valid form of payment for Amazon purchases. There will be **NO reimbursements for Amazon orders placed by personal cards of students or employees**.
- If your organization needs to make purchases via Amazon, submit a p-card request. FSE Student Engagement will review your request and notify you if it is approved. You would then make an appointment with the Business Office to use the Business Office Amazon account to make your purchase. Come prepared with a clear list of what you need.

## Successful speakers and/or performers

- If you plan to pay someone to speak or perform for your group, they will need to complete the following forms:
  - W-9 tax form
  - Invoice for services to organization
  - Independent Contractors form
- They may need to complete additional forms per ASU policies.
- If you plan to have a speaker or performer for your group, contact [fso@asu.edu](mailto:fso@asu.edu) at least a week prior to the date planned for the event so your FSO can be prepared to complete the paperwork in a timely manner.
