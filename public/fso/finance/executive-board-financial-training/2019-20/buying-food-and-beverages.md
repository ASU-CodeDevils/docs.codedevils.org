# Buying food and/or beverages

- Per ASU policies, if you plan to host an event on-campus, you must use [ASU-Approved Food Providers](https://eoss.asu.edu/mu/plan_event/food).
- For all purchases involving food or beverages that use ASU funding, you must complete the following paperwork:
  -  **Completed Business Meals Form (BMF)**. Requestor must sign the BMF.
  -  **Complete attendee roster** , includes event name, date, time, location, first and last names and university role (student, faculty, staff or industry).
  -  **Itemized receipts** for food and/or beverages; _no reimbursements for alcohol purchases._
- If you are not going to eat the food or beverages, put NOT FOR CONSUMPTION in the public purpose.

## Tipping anyone?

- As a general rule, stick with 10% or less.
- ALWAYS keep the receipt with the tip amount documented – no matter what kind of transaction it is (p-card, purchase order or reimbursement).
- Return all receipts to the Business Office within the following business day when purchasing food with the p-card.
