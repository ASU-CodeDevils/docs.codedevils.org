## Resources

Here are some resources for CodeDevils as an FSO:

- [CodeDevils on FSO](https://fso.engineering.asu.edu/project/codedevils-cd/) - The FSO site for CodeDevils.
- [Deans Funding](https://fso.engineering.asu.edu/deans-funding/?_ga=2.185142185.1027160660.1576376001-1659774083.1546800970) - Funding directly from Fulton allocated by the Dean
- [Event Planning](https://fso.engineering.asu.edu/toolbox/event-planning/?_ga=2.174784546.1027160660.1576376001-1659774083.1546800970) - Resources for planning events such as approved vendors, guides, and forms
- [FSO Directory](https://fso.engineering.asu.edu/student-org-directory/) - A listing of all student organizations registered through FSO
- [FSO on Canvas](https://canvas.asu.edu/courses/1083) - Get the most up-to-date FSO forms, quizzes and information
